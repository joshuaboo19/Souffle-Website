Common Cravings

Why 20 minutes for an order? +
Soufflé pancakes rely on air and low heat. If we rush them, they collapse. The 20-minute wait ensures yours are perfectly tall and jiggly!
Do you offer gluten-free options? +
Currently, we use premium wheat flour to achieve our signature height. We are working on a GF recipe—stay tuned!
Can I book a table? +
We are walk-in only to ensure everyone gets a fair chance at our daily 30-portion limit!
